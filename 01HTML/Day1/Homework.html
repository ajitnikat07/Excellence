Biography of Nikola Tesla

Early Life

Nikola Tesla was born on july 10,1856,in Smiljan,which was part of the Austrian Empire(modern-day Croatia).
His father was a priest,and his mother was an inventor who encoraged his curiosity from a young age.

Career

Tesla moved to the united States in 1884 to work for Thomos Edison.He later invented the alternating current(AC) electrical system,which laid the foundaion for modern electricity distribution
He also conducted pioneering experiments in wireless communication and X-rays.

Acheivements

Tesla held over 300 patents during his lifetime.He is best known for his contribution to the development of Ac power,the tesla coil,and wireless technologies
Despite his many contibutions,Tesla faced financial difficulties later in life.

Legacy

Tesla's work has left a lasting impact on modern technology.His invention have proved the way for many of the electrical systems we use today.
He is remembered as a visionary who was ahead of his time.
